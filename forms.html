Resource Panel

What types of requests fall under Technical Support and IT Services

This category should be selected for issues related to software problems, hardware malfunctions, network connectivity issues, and any other IT-related support.

When should I choose 'Human Resources' as my request category?

Select 'Human Resources' for inquiries or requests related to employee relations, company policies, payroll, benefits, hiring processes, and other HR-related matters.

What requests fall under the 'Finance and Administration' category?

This includes requests related to financial processes, budgeting, administrative assistance, office supplies, and any other financial or administrative services.

Which types of requests should go to 'Facilities and Administration'?

Choose this for requests related to office maintenance, facility management, safety issues, and general administrative support.

When to direct requests to the 'Sales and Marketing' department?

Use this category for requests associated with marketing campaigns, sales strategies, customer outreach, promotional activities, and other related marketing or sales tasks.

What should I consider as a 'Project Management' request?

This category is for requests related to project planning, coordination, resource allocation, timeline management, and other aspects of project management.

What constitutes a 'Customer Service' request?

Choose 'Customer Service' for requests pertaining to customer support, feedback management, client relations, and any other customer service-related activities.
